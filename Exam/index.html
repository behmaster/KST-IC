Sadie

Adopted May 13th, 2018

This is a fun tribute page to our loyal companions!

Sadie is a dog I rescued from Wayside Waifs in 2018. She is a medium sized dog, about 50 lbs. She loves every person she's ever met and doesn't really care about other dogs. Her favorite activities include going on walks, going to the dog park at Shawnee Mission Park, eating and getting pets.

  1. Has caught three squirrels
  2. Likes to be petted by three people at the same time
  3. Will eat any food besides citrus
